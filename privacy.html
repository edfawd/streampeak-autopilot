Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 July 2026

StreamPeak Autopilot is an independent desktop tool operated by the app owner. Privacy questions can be sent to emaadnoah09@outlook.de.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how StreamPeak Autopilot processes information when the account owner connects a TikTok account and uses the application to prepare or upload short-form video content.

2. Information processed

Depending on the permissions granted by the account owner, the application may process:

  • basic TikTok account information made available through the authorized API;
  • TikTok authorization data, such as access and refresh tokens;
  • video files, captions, titles, descriptions and publishing settings selected by the account owner;
  • technical logs needed to diagnose upload errors and prevent accidental duplicate uploads.

3. Purpose of processing

The information is used only to:

  • authenticate the account owner;
  • prepare and upload content requested or approved by the account owner;
  • check upload status and report errors;
  • protect the account against accidental duplicate posts.

4. Storage and security

The application is designed to store authorization data and generated media locally on the account owner’s computer. The operator does not sell personal information. Reasonable technical measures should be used to protect local files, including device passwords, operating-system updates and restricted access to credential files.

5. Sharing

Information is transmitted to TikTok only when needed to authenticate the account or carry out a requested upload or publishing action. TikTok processes that information under its own terms and privacy policy. Information may also be disclosed when required by applicable law.

6. Retention and deletion

Local authorization data and logs are retained until they are deleted by the account owner, are no longer required for the integration, or access is revoked. The account owner may revoke TikTok access through TikTok account settings and may delete the local token and log files.

7. User choices and rights

A user may request access, correction or deletion of information controlled by the operator, or object to processing where applicable. Requests can be sent to emaadnoah09@outlook.de. The user may also have the right to lodge a complaint with the competent data-protection authority.

8. Children

StreamPeak Autopilot is not intended to collect personal information from children. The connected TikTok account must comply with TikTok’s age and account requirements.

9. Changes

This policy may be updated when the application or legal requirements change. The current version will always be published on this page with a revised update date.
